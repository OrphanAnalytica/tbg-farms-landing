Why Choose TBG Farms?

TBG Farms crafts its feed with a focus on sustainable and local sourcing, featuring cassava meal as a primary energy source. We enrich this with other locally available ingredients, ensuring a well-rounded diet that supports the health and growth of our pigs.

Community Empowerment

Offering training programs and resources to support local farmers and foster economic growth.
